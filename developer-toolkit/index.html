 Pro-Change.

Blockchain Institute works to promote the adoption, development, and use of blockchain technology by providing free education to the public.

Blockchain Institute was established by Taylor Gerring, a Co-founder of the Ethereum Foundation; Andrew Gordon of Gordon Law Group; and Hannah Rosenberg, organizer of Chicago’s largest crypto community group—The Bitcoin and Open Blockchain Meetup.

We’re Building it Here.

Chicago has long been a center of financial innovation. From the origins of commodities trading to the creation of the dividend markets, the Windy City has been on the right side of every financial wave.

We’re here to make sure that a smooth transition to the Internet of Trust. Together, we can keep this city ahead.
